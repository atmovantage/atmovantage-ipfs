Now Accepting Payment in Crypto

Austin Shackelford

Love them or hate them, cryptocurrencies have been disrupting traditional banking and finance for over a decade now. Although the price is often volatile AtmoVantage believes that crypto is the future. Today I'm proud to announce that customers who want to engage with AtmoVantage can easily make payments using either Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum or USD Coin.

The three WordPress annual maintenance plans are all available to purchase directly from the new Crypto Shop. Simply select the maintenance plan you want to purchase for your website, choose either Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum or USDC, and send the specified amount to the address on the order page. Once the transaction has been confirmed on the blockchain your order will be marked as complete.

AtmoVantage also provides a method for paying any invoice using crypto. If you're an AtmoVantage customer that received an invoice, such as for custom website design services, just go to the Invoice Crypto Payment page, enter the invoice amount in USD, and proceed to checkout using your preferred cryptocurrency. Don't forget to enter the invoice number in the Customer Notes section during checkout so it's easy to match your payment. Once your payment is confirmed your invoice will be marked as "Paid".

Despite the volatile nature of crypto I'm a firm believer in the fundamental technology of the blockchain. Time will tell whether Bitcoin or other alternative cryptocurrencies will become the dominant players in the crypto markets. Depending on customer preferences I may expand the available crypto options beyond the top 4 (Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum and USDC). Which cryptocurrency would you like to pay with and why?
